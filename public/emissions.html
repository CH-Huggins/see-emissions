More Info:

Have you ever wondered how much emissions are when mentioned in an article? Far too often do news sites use numbers in esoteric ways that exclude the general population. Well now, you have a place to take those numbers to get more information on them.

Here is where you can enter your emissions, either in the units of tonnes of CO2 or in electricity usage (kWh). These numbers can typically be found in news articles or scientific journals. There are other sources for these numbers, but this is where you will most normally find them.

Enter the number you want to research in the text box, and select the form of emissions that you would like to check on. Then click submit and there you go, the emission's comparisons will be provided.
