See Emissions

See Emissions was created in order to present pollution in a more understandable way. Climate change conversations are based around science which automatically removes a good bit of the population from the discussion. However, in order to combat climate chage, we need to all be united. So given CO2 emissions or energy usage, you can come here and find out how that actually compares to other activities or people.
